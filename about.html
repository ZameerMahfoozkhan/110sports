About 110SPORTS

Your trusted partner for premium cricket equipment — Official 7070 Sports Dealer

Our Story

110SPORTS is a modern cricket and sportswear brand, proudly serving as an official dealer of the renowned 7070 Sports (7T7T). Founded with a bold vision to become a leading name in India's sportswear industry, we have grown from a passion project into a nationally recognized brand known for innovation, performance, and style.

With operations backed by 7070 Sports' modern 8,000+ sq. ft. manufacturing facility in the Bhiwandi-Kalyan industrial zone, equipped with advanced technology and skilled craftsmanship, we deliver high-quality, performance-driven sportswear tailored to meet the needs of athletes and active individuals across the country.

Today, 7T7T proudly serves customers through a vast retail network of over 500 stores across India, and 110SPORTS is committed to bringing these trusted products with a premium, modern experience to cricket enthusiasts nationwide and internationally.

Our Mission

"To provide cricket enthusiasts across India and worldwide with the finest quality cricket equipment at affordable prices, while promoting the sport and nurturing young talent in our community."
